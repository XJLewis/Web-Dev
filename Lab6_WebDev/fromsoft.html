About FromSoftware

FromSoftware is a Japanese game development company that develops tough-as-nails action games. The creators of several hits such as the Dark Souls series, Elden Ring, Bloodborne, and many more, "FromSoftware" has become synonymous with high quality and difficult video games. FromSoftware's first game with their iconic gameplay system was "Demon's Souls", released in 2009 for the PlayStation 3. Before this, they were known mostly for their King's Field and Armored Core games, which while still tough, felt very different compared to their current games. After Demon's Souls came Dark Souls, a multiplatform hit that cemented their signature formula. FromSoftware would go on to make 2 other games in the series, and games mimicking similar systems would be called "souls-likes". Between Dark Souls 2 and 3 FromSoftware made their first game outside of the Knight-and-dragon fantasy style with Bloodeborne, exclusive for the PlayStation 4 (though fans have been begging for a PC port). With Bloodborne, FromSoft showed that they could still make amazing, diverse worlds. FromSoftware proved this again with the more linear and limited (but still super challenging and expertly crafted) "Sekiro: Shadows Die Twice". In 2022, FromSoftware released their most ambitious game to date: Elden Ring. Elden Ring was FromSoftware's first open-world soulslike, and it has sold more than any other game they've released to date. Elden Ring won the 2022 Game of the Year award, and the DLC, Shadow of the Erdree, is also their largest, most ambitious expansion to a game.

So, make sure you keep up with this site for all the newest FromSoftware news! We can't wait for their next project, and we hope you feel the same!
