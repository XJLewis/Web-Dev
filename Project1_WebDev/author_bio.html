Biography

The man, the myth, the legend. Xander Lewis is the hit breakthrough author of 5 bestsellers in just the last year! Learn about the Author that NYT is calling The Gen Z Homer (of Odyssey fame, not Simpson.)

Mr. Lewis grew up in Brevard County, Florida. At 14 years old, he moved to Monroe County, West Virinia. He currently studies Computer Information Systems at Conocord University in Athens, West Virginia and writes award-winning novels in his free time. Explore the pages below to learn more about these wonderful stories such as "A Comprehensive History of Paperclips", "The Science of Drying Paint: An In-Depth Exploration" and the all new “The Silent World of Office Carpeting: A Texture Analysis”

So strap on your seatbelts and get ready for an epic collection of exciting stories to knock your socks off!
